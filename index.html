Velo Tour Festival: a collective 3-day bicycle journey in Belgium

Save the date: the first Velo Tour Festival will take place from Friday 1 to Sunday 3 May 2020!

100 cycling enthusiasts from all over Belgium will gather to make a 3-day bicycle tour in Belgium, share their excitement about bike touring and have a great time together. This festival is for not-for-profit and entirely organised by a group of volunteers. Our website with all further details will be launched by January 2020.

So, is it already marked in your agenda? ;)
